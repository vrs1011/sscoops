Policies

------ Financing ------

All major credit cards accepted.
Cash & debit cards accepted.
Apple pay accepted.

------ Delivery ------

Hard serve flavors can be ordered in a pint and delivered.
All deliveries must be done via Door Dash, GrubHub, or UberEats. S-Scoop's does not provide delivery.

------ Return Policies ------

All ice cream sales are final. If you are unhappy with your ice cream, please return it for inspection.
Refunds will only be given if the ice cream is deemed inedible.
